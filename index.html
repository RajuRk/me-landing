    About Us

    Surrounded by an expanse of lush green environment, Rathinam Technical College imparts quality education and prepares its students to think creatively, critically and innovatively. Well built infrastructure and academic excellence has granted the NAAC accreditation and four star status to the institution. Rathinam is the only engineering college that provides a variety of courses with a number of specializations and disciplines. The college caters a large student community that witnesses its students acquiring job offers with enviable positions in India and aboard. The college is making steady progress through inventive methods and a team of dedicated faculty work towards achieving the vision of the institution.
